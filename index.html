PAKISTAN AND ITS NEIGHBOURING COUNTRIES

PAKISTAN:

Pakistan is present in South Asia and is also known as ISLAMIC REPUBLIC OF PAKISTAN.It is a muslim country. People of pakistan follows the religion ISLAM. Pakistan is the 5th most populous country in the world ,with a population of 241.5 million people.It comes to existance on 14 August 1947.It is called independance day of Pakistan.Pakistan is declared a nuclear weapons state and growth-leading economies.It's currency is the Pakistani Ruppee.National language of Pakistan is Urdu. Karachi is the biggest city of Pakistan and Islamabad is it's capital.
It's four neighbouring countries are: 1.India.
2.China.
3.Afghanistan.
4.Iran.

INDIA:

India is present in South Asia and is also known as REPUBLIC OF INDIA.India is the most populous country in the world,with a population of 1.408 billion people. Majority of indian follows Hinduism religion. India is famous for its culture,foods,arts,traditions and music style. India currency is the Indian Ruppee.National language of India is Hindi.

CHINA:

China is present in East Asia and is also known as PEOPLE REPUBLIC OF CHINA.It is the second most populous country with a population of 1.4 billion. It is the largest manufacture and exporter of goods.Majority of China follows Bddhism religion. China currency is the Chinese Yuan.National language of China is Chinese.

AFGHANISTAN:

Afghanistan is present at crossroads of Central Asia and South Asia and is also known as ISLAMIC EMIRATE OF AFGHANISTAN.It is a muslim country. People of Afghanistan follows the religion ISLAM.It is well known for the growth pf fine-fruits. Afghanistan currency is the Afghan Afghani. National language of Afghanistan is Dari.

IRAN:

Iran is present in West Asia and is also known as PERSIA officially known as ISLAMIC REPUBLIC OF IRAN. Most of the Iranians follows Shiism religion. Iran is famous for art,literature,poetry,music,cuisine and architecture. Iran currency is the Iranian Rial.National language of Iran is Persian.
